Pricing FAQ

Yes! All our packages can be customized to fit your specific needs and budget. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What payment terms do you offer?

We offer flexible payment terms: 50% deposit to start, balance upon completion. Other arrangements available for large projects.

Are there hidden costs?

No! Our pricing is transparent. You'll know all costs upfront. Only additional work requested beyond the scope may incur extra charges.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your website, app, or support plan at any time. We'll calculate the upgrade cost fairly.

Do you offer discounts for bulk projects?

Yes! We offer special discounts for organizations needing multiple services. Reach out to discuss your requirements.

What's included in "support"?

Support includes bug fixes, technical assistance, minor updates, and help with your system. Major enhancements are charged separately.
